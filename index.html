Intro

Scott is a professional musician located in Austin, Texas. His specialty is a synergistic blend of Classical, Jazz, and Contemporary solo guitar.


Master classes with Manuel Barrueco, Pat Metheny, Dominic Miller (guitarist for Sting) were highlights while earning his PhD at the University of North Texas.

Scott offers lessons at his Guitar Studio in Austin, TX.
